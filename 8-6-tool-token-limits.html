Screaming Frog Limits

License Restrictions

  • Free Version: Limited to 500 URLs per crawl
  • Licensed Version: Unlimited URLs
  • Automation: Scheduling requires licensed version

Risk Mitigation

  • Ensure proper licensing is maintained
  • Optimize crawl configurations to avoid unnecessary crawling
  • Use list mode for targeted crawls
  • Monitor crawl duration and resource usage
