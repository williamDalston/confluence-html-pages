Power BI Basics

This page provides an introduction to Power BI basics for new team members. Learn fundamental concepts and skills needed to work with Power BI in HHS Analytics.

What is Power BI?

Power BI is a business intelligence tool for creating interactive dashboards, reports, and data visualizations. It enables data-driven decision-making through intuitive visualizations and self-service analytics.

Key Concepts

  • Datasets: Data models and connections
  • Reports: Interactive visualizations and pages
  • Dashboards: Collections of visuals from multiple reports
  • Power Query: Data transformation and preparation (M language)
  • DAX: Data Analysis Expressions for calculations and measures
