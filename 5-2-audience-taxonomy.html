Audience Taxonomy (with Adrian's Personas)

This page documents the audience taxonomy and personas developed by Adrian for HHS content strategy. Understanding audience segments is critical for content analytics and targeted reporting.

Audience Segments

Primary audience segments include:

  • General Public: Broad public audience seeking information
  • Healthcare Providers: Medical professionals and practitioners
  • Researchers: Academic and research community
  • Policy Makers: Government officials and policy developers
  • Media: Journalists and media professionals

Persona Definitions

Adrian's personas provide detailed profiles including:

  • Demographics and characteristics
  • Information needs and goals
  • Content preferences and consumption patterns
  • Pain points and challenges

Content Tagging

Content should be tagged with appropriate audience taxonomy to enable:

  • Audience-specific content performance analysis
  • Content gap identification
  • Targeted content recommendations
  • Personalization opportunities
