Metrics Cadence (Weekly, Monthly, Quarterly)

This page documents the reporting cadence for different metrics and dashboards, ensuring regular updates and timely insights across HHS Analytics.

Weekly Metrics

  • Social Media Engagement: Follower counts, engagement rates, content performance
  • Service Desk Metrics: Ticket volume, resolution times, SLA compliance
  • Website Traffic: Page views, unique visitors, top pages

Monthly Metrics

  • Website Quality Score: Technical SEO, content quality, accessibility
  • Content Performance: Top content, engagement metrics, content inventory
  • Team Performance: Team SLA compliance, throughput, workload distribution
  • Full Site Crawl Results: Complete website audit and analysis

Quarterly Metrics

  • Strategic KPIs: High-level performance indicators
  • Trend Analysis: Long-term trends and patterns
  • Goal Tracking: Progress toward quarterly goals
  • Executive Summary: Leadership dashboard updates

Ad-Hoc Reporting

Some metrics are available on-demand:

  • Real-time dashboard views
  • Custom analysis requests
  • Special projects and initiatives

Reporting Schedule

  • Weekly Reports: Monday mornings
  • Monthly Reports: First week of month
  • Quarterly Reports: First week of quarter

Stakeholder Distribution

Reports are distributed to:

  • Analytics team members
  • Relevant stakeholders (see Section 8-2)
  • Leadership for executive summaries
