HHS Records Management Training

This page documents HHS records management training requirements and resources for analytics team members. Proper records management ensures compliance with federal regulations.

Training Requirements

Team members must complete:

  • HHS Records Management Training
  • Annual refresher training
  • Role-specific training as needed

Key Topics

  • Records retention schedules
  • Records disposal procedures
  • Electronic records management
  • Compliance requirements
  • Best practices

Resources

  • HHS Records Management website
  • Training materials and guides
  • Contact information for records management office
  • FAQ and troubleshooting resources

Compliance

Ensure all analytics work complies with:

  • HHS records management policies
  • Federal records management regulations
  • Data retention requirements
  • Disposal procedures

Documentation

Document training completion and compliance status. Maintain records of:

  • Training completion dates
  • Certifications and credentials
  • Compliance reviews and audits
