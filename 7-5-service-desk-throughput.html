Service Desk Throughput

This page documents service desk throughput metrics, tracking ticket volume, resolution times, and team productivity in the HHS service desk.

Throughput Metrics

  • Ticket Volume: Number of tickets created over time
  • Resolution Rate: Tickets resolved per time period
  • Average Resolution Time: Mean time to resolve tickets
  • Backlog Size: Number of open tickets at any time
  • Throughput Rate: Tickets completed per day/week

Team Performance

  • Tickets assigned per team member
  • Resolution time by team
  • SLA compliance by team
  • Workload distribution

Trend Analysis

Throughput trends help identify:

  • Seasonal patterns in ticket volume
  • Changes in resolution times
  • Backlog growth or reduction
  • Team capacity and workload

Data Sources

Metrics are calculated from:

  • Fact_TicketHistory model (Section 6-2)
  • Jira ticket data (Section 6-1)
  • Team SLA Dashboard (Section 6-3)

Reporting

Throughput metrics are included in:

  • Team SLA Dashboard (Section 6-3)
  • Executive Summary Visuals (Section 7-6)
  • Analytics Roadmap reporting (Section 8)
