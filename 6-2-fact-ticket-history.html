Fact_TicketHistory Model

This page documents the Fact_TicketHistory star schema model for Jira ticket tracking and workflow analytics in Power BI.

Model Overview

Fact_TicketHistory captures ticket status changes and transitions over time, enabling analysis of ticket lifecycles, SLA compliance, and team performance.

Fact Table Structure

Fact_TicketHistory

  • TicketHistoryID: Surrogate key (primary key)
  • TicketKey: Foreign key to Dim_Ticket
  • DateKey: Foreign key to Dim_Date
  • StatusFromKey: Foreign key to Dim_Status
  • StatusToKey: Foreign key to Dim_Status
  • TeamKey: Foreign key to Dim_Team
  • AssigneeKey: Foreign key to Dim_User
  • TimeInStatus: Minutes in previous status
  • SLACompliant: Boolean flag (1 or 0)
  • Timestamp: Status change timestamp

Dimension Tables

  • Dim_Ticket: Ticket attributes (key, type, priority, component)
  • Dim_Date: Date dimension for time-based analysis
  • Dim_Status: Status values and categories
  • Dim_Team: Team structure and organization
  • Dim_User: User/assignee information

Key Measures

  • Ticket Count: Total number of tickets
  • Average Time in Status: Mean time tickets spend in each status
  • SLA Compliance Rate: Percentage of tickets meeting SLA
  • Resolution Time: Time from creation to resolution
