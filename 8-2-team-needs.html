Team Analytics Needs & Requirements

This page documents analytics needs and requirements for each team and stakeholder, ensuring alignment between analytics deliverables and business needs.

Team Needs by Area

Service Desk & UXD

  • Role/Area: Service Desk metrics, UXD outcomes, website quality
  • Analytics Needs: Ticket throughput & SLAs, website plain language/readability, UX outcomes
  • Key Metrics: Ticket throughput, SLA compliance, website quality scores
  • Reporting Cadence: Monthly service desk dashboard

Analytics Engineering (BigQuery, Looker)

  • Role/Area: Google Analytics → BigQuery → Looker pipeline
  • Analytics Needs: Coordinate Power BI and Looker to complement each other
  • Key Metrics: GA4 data pipeline health, Looker dashboard performance
  • Reporting Cadence: Ongoing coordination and alignment

Social Media Analytics

  • Role/Area: Social Analytics / Sprinklr
  • Analytics Needs: Social media performance tracking, engagement metrics
  • Key Metrics: Follower growth, engagement rates, content performance
  • Reporting Cadence: Weekly social trends dashboard

Website Crawling & Metadata

  • Role/Area: Website Crawling / Screaming Frog
  • Analytics Needs: Crawl scheduling, historical data tracking
  • Key Metrics: Crawl success rate, data quality scores
  • Reporting Cadence: Monthly crawl reports
