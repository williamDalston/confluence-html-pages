This page documents the current topic taxonomy structure and proposed improvements for better content organization and analytics in HHS.

Current Taxonomy

The current topic taxonomy includes:

  • Top-level categories (e.g., Health Topics, Services, Programs)
  • Sub-categories and sub-topics
  • Topic tags and keywords

Proposed Improvements

Proposed enhancements to the taxonomy structure:

  • More granular topic classification
  • Consistent hierarchical structure
  • Cross-referencing and related topics
  • Better alignment with search behavior

Benefits of Taxonomy Updates

  • Improved content discoverability
  • Better content performance analysis
  • Enhanced search functionality
  • More accurate content recommendations
