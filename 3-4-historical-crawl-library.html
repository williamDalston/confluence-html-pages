Historical Crawl Library

This page maintains a library of historical website crawls, enabling trend analysis and comparison over time. Historical data helps identify changes, improvements, and areas needing attention.

Crawl Archive

Historical crawls are organized by:

  • Date: Monthly or quarterly crawls
  • Type: Full site audit, quick health check, or custom extraction
  • Purpose: SEO audit, content inventory, metadata analysis, etc.

Key Metrics Tracked

  • Total number of pages crawled
  • Broken links and errors
  • Metadata completeness (title, description, etc.)
  • Content quality indicators
  • SEO performance metrics

Accessing Historical Data

Historical crawl data is stored in:

  • CSV/Excel exports in the Upload Area (Section 3-5)
  • Power BI datasets for trend visualization
  • Documented crawl reports in Confluence

Usage Guidelines

  • Maintain consistent crawl configuration for comparable results
  • Document any changes in crawl settings or scope
  • Regular cadence: monthly or quarterly depending on site activity
  • Archive older crawls but maintain access for historical analysis
