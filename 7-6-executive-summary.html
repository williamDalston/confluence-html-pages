Executive Summary Visuals (Mock PBIs)

This page contains mockups and specifications for Executive Summary visuals in Power BI. These high-level dashboards provide leadership with key insights and KPIs at a glance.

Executive Dashboard Overview

The Executive Summary provides:

  • High-level KPI overview
  • Key trends and insights
  • Performance indicators across all areas
  • Strategic metrics and goals tracking

Key Visualizations

  • KPI Scorecard: Key metrics at a glance
  • Trend Lines: Performance trends over time
  • Comparison Charts: Current vs. previous period
  • Status Indicators: Green/yellow/red status indicators
  • Top/Bottom Lists: Best and worst performing areas

Metrics Included

  • Website Quality Score (Section 7-1)
  • Engagement metrics (Section 7-3)
  • Social media performance (Section 7-4)
  • Service desk throughput (Section 7-5)
  • Content performance indicators

Design Principles

  • Clear, concise visualizations
  • Minimal text, maximum visual impact
  • Color-coded status indicators
  • Drill-down capability for details
