Key Power BI Capabilities for Our Work

Power Query (M Language): Transform and clean data from APIs, CSV files, and databases. Essential for standardizing data from Screaming Frog crawls, Sprinklr API responses, and Jira exports before they enter our data models.
Data Modeling (Star Schema): Organize data into fact tables (metrics) and dimension tables (context) for efficient analysis. Our templates support ticket history, page views, social engagement, and content performance metrics.
DAX (Data Analysis Expressions): Create calculated measures and columns for complex analytics. Examples include website quality scores, SLA compliance rates, engagement trends, and content reading level calculations.
Interactive Visualizations: Build charts, maps, tables, and custom visuals that respond to user interactions. Dashboards enable drill-down from executive summaries to detailed page-level analytics.
Automated Refresh: Schedule data updates to keep dashboards current. Critical for monthly/quarterly reporting cadence and real-time monitoring of website quality and service desk performance.

Our Power BI Workflow

  1. Data Collection: Extract data from APIs (Sprinklr, Jira), exports (Screaming Frog), and databases (GA4, Drupal)
  2. Data Transformation: Use Power Query to clean, standardize, and shape data according to our star schema templates
  3. Data Modeling: Build fact and dimension tables, establish relationships, and create calculated measures using DAX
  4. Visualization: Design interactive dashboards with charts, tables, and KPIs that tell the story of our data
  5. Publishing & Sharing: Deploy dashboards to Power BI Service, configure automated refresh schedules, and share with stakeholders
  6. Iteration & Improvement: Gather feedback, refine metrics, and enhance dashboards based on stakeholder needs and changing requirements
